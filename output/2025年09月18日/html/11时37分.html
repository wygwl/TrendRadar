The Fed’s dual mandate—low inflation and a healthy labor market—has created tension in decision-making. <br/>- <b>Core Inflation</b>: Predicted to rise to 3.1% by the end of 2025, with expectations for a gradual decline to 2.6% by the end of 2026.
